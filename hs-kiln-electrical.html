HS Kiln & Electrical

In Progress
About This Project

Replacement of one of the high school ceramics kilns—both approximately 30 years old and heavily rusted—along with the electrical upgrades needed to support the new unit. The project will restore full ceramics production capacity for HS art classes and ensure the electrical infrastructure meets code requirements for the new equipment.

Construction Impact
Work Hours & Noise Impact

Electrical work will require a brief shutdown of power to the ceramics room and adjacent circuits. This will be scheduled during non-instructional periods.
