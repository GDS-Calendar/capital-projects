LMS Band Room Stairs & Conductor Stand

About This Project

Modular rise stairs in the band room providing a safe pathway for percussionists to access the upper risers without cutting through other sections during rehearsals. The project also includes a conductor stand to improve visibility and podium organization during ensemble rehearsals.
