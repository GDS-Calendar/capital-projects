New Deans Suite & Offices

Planned
About This Project

Conversion of a first-floor High School classroom into a new administrative suite comprising three private offices and a dedicated focus room, totaling approximately 655 square feet of renovated space. New doors provide direct access from the student lounge, positioning the deans team at the heart of student life. The redesigned space will provide a more functional, accessible, and private environment for student support conversations.
