LMS Field Storage Shed

In Progress
About This Project

A dedicated storage shed on the LMS field for Lower School, Middle School, and PE equipment, keeping recess and athletics gear organized, secure, and accessible for daily use. The shed will provide weather-protected storage directly adjacent to the field, eliminating the need to transport equipment long distances from interior storage areas.

Construction Impact
Work Hours & Noise Impact

Installation work will take place on the LMS field perimeter and is expected to have minimal impact on daily school operations.
