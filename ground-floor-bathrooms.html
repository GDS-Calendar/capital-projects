Ground Floor Bathrooms

Planned
About This Project

Renovation of the High School ground floor restrooms, creating new single-user accessible bathrooms with both direct outdoor access and interior access options. The redesigned layout introduces a reception area with card reader access controls and a new door with privacy film sidelights for improved security and user comfort. New signage and updated finishes will create a more welcoming and functional facility for students, staff, and visitors.
