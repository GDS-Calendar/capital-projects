Unified Nurses Suite

Planned
About This Project

Renovation and expansion of the Lower/Middle School nurse's suite to create a unified, purpose-built health space. The new configuration includes two single private offices, a dedicated waiting area, three beds, a new base cabinet with sink, and glass sidelights at both new entry doors for improved visibility and safety. Archive storage will be relocated to LMS Level 2 to maximize functional space within the suite. The project consolidates school health services into a more professional, accessible, and private environment.
